Hiya — I'm Reon, a DevSecOps engineer who spends way too much time thinking about pipelines, cloud platforms, and how to fix things when they break.

After two years working with Azure DevOps and cloud infrastructure at Kyndryl in Bengaluru, I packed my bags and moved to the UK to dive deeper into security through an MSc in Applied Cyber Security at Queen's University Belfast. Lately, I've been focused on the space where cloud, automation, and security actually meet — building systems that are fast to ship but hard to break.

I specialize in CI/CD automation, Infrastructure as Code, and shifting security left so it's baked in from day one rather than bolted on at the end. My favorite kind of work involves taking messy, manual, fragile processes and turning them into clean, automated, reliable ones.

Off the clock, you'll find me messing around in a home lab breaking things to understand how they work, half-watching football, or falling down a browsing rabbit hole at 2am (no regrets).
